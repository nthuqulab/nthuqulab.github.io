    • 我們構建一個量子預言機模型，稱為平行T-depth查詢迭代量子計算模
    型，其中T表示在每一次量子計算迭代中允許使用的預言機查詢次數上限。
    在此模型下，我們證明：對於任意量子近似計數演算法，當輸入為包含N
    個元素的清單、其中有M個被標記元素，且乘性誤差為ϵ=| ˜M−M|/M 時，其查詢複雜度至少為Ω (N/ϵMT)。

    We construct a quantum oracle model called the parallel T-depth query iterative
    quantum computational model, where T is the number of oracle queries allowed
    in each iteration of the quantum computation. In this model, we prove that any
    quantum approximate counting algorithm, given input size N (the total number
    of items in the list), M (the number of marked items), and multiplicative error
    ϵ = | ˜M−M|/M , musthavequery complexity at least Ω(N/ϵMT).
